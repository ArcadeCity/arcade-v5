At this weekend's [pleb.fi Austin](https://pleb.fi/austin/) Bitcoin hackathon,
we built a prototype for [OnlySats](https://github.com/ArcadeCity/onlysats) &mdash;
a basic version of OnlyFans powered by the [Lightning Network](https://tftc.io/martys-bent/issue-1061/).

We were excited to [win third place](https://twitter.com/JeremyRubin/status/1429624590255415299)
&mdash; but more importantly, excited to connect with other teams already building
infrastructure to support a full-fledged Bitcoin version of OnlyFans.

Arcade City won't be leading the charge on this one. But we are intent on helping it
come to fruition.

The Bitcoin community must send a message to sex workers, and to everyone vulnerable or
uncertain about how to make a stable living:

**If you fall out of the old economy, we will catch you.**

Stay tuned.
